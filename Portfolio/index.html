Welcome to my online site!

The world is changing at an incredible speed. In fact some people say we are experiencing the fourth industrial revolution. This revolution is largely driven by computer information processing technology, which is an amazing force. Artificial intelligence has become the biggest booster of the current revolution from a high academic research field. We witness the impact of artificial intelligence on our lives every day from medicine to agriculture and all aspects. But we are still in the early stages and have a lot of unfinished challenges. As a student in the College of Information Sciences and Technology I am also curious about the field of artificial intelligence.

At the same time, I am also very interested in historical sites. When I visited these places I was deeply shocked by human civilization and the wisdom of working people.

We can't forget the past, we look forward to the future, we should focus on the present.
